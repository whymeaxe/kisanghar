Our Story

Straight from the kisaan's field to your glass

Kisaan Ghar started as a promise: the fruit in your shake should taste like the fruit on the tree, not a syrup poured to imitate it. We buy sitafal, mango and jamun in season, straight from growers we know by name, and turn it into lassi, shreekhand, faluda and kulfi the same day.

There's no cold-storage pulp on our shelves and no artificial flavouring in our kitchen — just fruit, milk, rabdi and the patience to make it properly. Pull up a bench on the green lawn under the cane lanterns, and taste the difference.

The Space

Bamboo roof, green lawn, open air

A woven bamboo ceiling, cane pendant lights and a stretch of lawn to sit on — Kisaan Ghar is built to linger in, not rush through.

Bamboo Ceiling Seating

Cane pendant lamps and a hand-woven roof over the main seating lawn.

Open Lawn
